A BRIEF HISTORY ABOUT Y2K15 SET IN MADONNA

It all started in September, 2012 where a lot of vibrant young individauls (male and female) came from different parts of Nigeria to be one family in a local government area called Ihitte/Uboma located in Imo state, Nigeria. They lived happily in same environments, sharing most of the facilities provided by the higher authorities. Despite challenges faced by seniors, teachers and cultist from far and wide we still came out with flying colors and today we can be proud to say we are among the successful set Madonna Science School Ihitte/Uboma have ever produced. May we continue to grow together helping one another attain greater heights and successful career path in life so at the end we will have a greater cause to celebrate.
